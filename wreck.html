SMASHCAR

Rocket-powered trucks, one giant ball, two goals — and full-contact demolition. Smash the ball into the enemy goal. Smash the enemy while you're at it. First to 5 goals wins. Nobody falls off, nobody's eliminated.

  • W/S drive · A/D steer · Shift boost · Space jump (again in air = flip-dodge) · R flip upright
  • Phone: hold the left pad to drive, slide to steer, pull down to reverse · right-side BOOST / JUMP / FLIP
  • Destruction is a power play: ram enemies to crush real dents in, tear off their bumpers and wheels — a battered truck is slow and pulls sideways. Beat one to 0% hull and it's DEMOLISHED — gone for 3 seconds while you attack the open net.
  • Powerup pads spawn around the court: REPAIR, OVERDRIVE (8s of raw speed), SHIELD (bolts armor plates all around your truck that soak hits and shear off when cracked). Wrecks drop salvage to patch your hull. Everyone gets a fresh truck at every kickoff.
  • HOT BALL: hard strikes heat it up — a white-hot ball wrecks whatever it slams into. Hitting the ball is always safe; standing in front of a screamer is not. Goals cool it off.
  • Demolitions and mega-shots blast craters in the pitch — by the late game you're playing on a battlefield.
