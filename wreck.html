Rocket-powered trucks, one giant ball, two goals — and full-contact demolition. Smash the ball into the enemy goal. Smash the enemy while you're at it. First to 5 goals wins. Nobody falls off, nobody's eliminated.

  • W/S drive · A/D steer · Shift boost · Space jump (again in air = flip-dodge) · R flip upright
  • Phone: hold the left pad to drive, slide to steer, pull down to reverse · right-side BOOST / JUMP / FLIP
