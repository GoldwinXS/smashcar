'MERICA · CARS · ROCKETS · 100-truck battle royale

CRUMB ISLAND

100 monster trucks rocket-drop onto a huge mountain island. Steer your fall, hit the dirt, and loot — an item's color is how good it is (common → uncommon → rare → epic → MYTHIC). Wreck rivals to make their gear burst out. Ram, flip, and outlast the storm.

  • Drive: auto-accel, steer ←/→ (or A/D), brake ↓/S, powerslide hold C. Boost = Space — works in mid-air to fly where you point; it drains fast, so refill on the glowing boost pads all over the map (they recharge).
  • Rocket League moves: jump Shift; in the air tap jump again to dodge — forward = speed burst, ←/→ = side-dodge, back = half-flip (snap 180). Press jump just before landing to wavedash. Hold C to drift; a long slide grants a release boost. Double-jump + air control on ramps & jump pads.
  • Loot — just four items, and rarity (the color) = how BIG the boost is: common→uncommon→rare→epic→MYTHIC. Speed (top speed), Boost (longer-lasting / faster-refilling boost), and Jump (higher jumps) all stack & show on your truck. Pickups don't respawn — kills drop the loot.
  • Seeking Rocket (the only gadget): fire with F. It locks the nearest enemy in front of you and homes — but it turns slowly, so a target that jukes or boosts can escape it. ~38 damage, not a one-shot. Rarer pickups give more rockets.
