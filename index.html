Comet Court

Goal: be the last comet on the platform. You are the cyan comet. Slingshot yourself into the 3 rivals and shove them off the edge — but don't get shoved off yourself.

  • Aim: press & drag back from your cyan comet, release to launch (like a slingshot). Pull farther = more power.
  • Knock them off: hit a rival on its inner side so it flies out past the glowing edge. That's a knockout.
  • The swirls are hazards, not goals — gravity wells curve every shot. The dotted line shows your path.
  • Take turns: you flick, then each rival flicks. Last comet standing wins the round; the platform then shrinks.
