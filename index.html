THE PASS

Place a dab of your sauce on any open tile. You (cyan) win the instant your sauce forms one unbroken line from the left edge to the right. The rival cat (orange) is racing to link top to bottom.

  • It can't end in a draw — once the board fills, exactly one of you has crossed. So every tile you take also blocks the rival. Attack and defend are the same move.
  • No two games are alike: there's no formula or endgame trick — you have to read the whole board, every time.
  • Watch for double-threats: leave two ways to bridge a gap and the rival can only block one.

This is a connection game (the Hex/Y family) — the maths says these are the deepest simple games: PSPACE-complete, with no closed-form solution and no solved endgame, so they stay a fresh puzzle every game (the fix for the "samey" parity games before this). An honest, obscure classic, reskinned as ours.
