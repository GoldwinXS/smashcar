SIMMER

Every pot has a doneness number. Stir a pot and it — plus its four neighbours — each drop by one. Any pot that hits exactly 0 is plated in your colour. When the stove is empty, the most dishes wins. The Capybara of Indifference judges, unmoved.

  • Your move: click any cooking pot to stir it (a plus-shaped pulse). Then it's the rival cat's turn.
  • The catch: stirring also nudges pots toward 0 for whoever stirs next — so leaving a pot on 1 hands the rival a free dish. Set up multi-plates for yourself; deny them to the rival.
  • Grabbing the most dishes this stir isn't always right — sometimes you stir to deny, not to plate.

An original mechanic (not a known game), with a combinatorial-game core: it's adversarial and a purely greedy "plate the most now" player loses ~75% of games to a thinking one (verified in simulation). Theme from 2026's biggest internet obsessions: AI + cats + cooking.
