Comet Court

Goal: be the last comet on the platform. You are the cyan comet. You and the rivals take turns flicking — last one on the platform wins.

  • Aim: drag back from your comet and release, like a slingshot. Farther = more power. The dotted line previews your curve through the gravity wells.
  • PULSE (Space / button): your defense. A rival's shot is shown as a dashed line before it fires — tap PULSE to shove it away, or to knock a nearby rival off the edge yourself. One charge per round.
  • Play the angles: rivals go after whoever is most exposed. Hang back from the edge and let them shove each other off.
  • Perks stack each round — build a tank, a sniper, or a pulse-bomber.
