FIREWALL

Viruses cross the grid toward your core on the right. Every virus shows exactly where it will move next — so each turn is a planning puzzle, not a reflex test. Place programs, funnel the swarm into kill-zones, and don't let them breach.

Each turn: spend energy to place or recycle programs, then hit Execute. Your programs fire first, then every virus advances one step along the arrow. Survive 8 waves.

Tip: a Wall reroutes the swarm — bend their path through a Nova or a Mine. You can place and recycle freely while planning; nothing is locked until you Execute.
