Fortnite × Rocket League · car battle royale

CRUMB ISLAND

Twelve cat-trucks rocket-drop onto a big hilly island. Steer your fall, land, then loot upgrades that bolt visibly onto your truck — armor, spikes, turbo, grip. Ram rivals off the cliffs, grab the high ground, and outlast the closing storm. Last truck running wins.

  • Drop: steer with ←/→ (or A/D) as you fall; you land where you aim.
  • Drive: auto-accelerate, steer, brake ↓/S, jump Shift off ramps, boost Space.
  • Upgrades (cube pickups) stack and show on your truck: armor = hull, spikes = ram, turbo = speed, grip = handling. Plus boost/repair/shield/shock orbs.
