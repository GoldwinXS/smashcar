Drift-racing × territory painting

DRIFT

Drift your cat around the plate, smearing sauce. The floor belongs to whoever painted it most recently — and sauce fades, so you have to keep moving and keep re-claiming. Most plate covered when the timer runs out wins.

  • Steer: move your mouse / finger and your cat drifts toward it (it has momentum — lean into the slides).
  • Paint & re-paint: drive over a rival's turf to flip it to yours. It's a constant tug-of-war over area.
  • Three rival cats are doing the same. No upgrades, no menus — just claim the most plate.

Mash-up of real-time drift-racing and territory control; the scoring is a live area / nearest-claim partition (the Voronoi idea from earlier, made real-time and adversarial so there's no greedy solution).
