Fortnite × Rocket League · car battle royale

CRUMB ISLAND

Eight cat-trucks dropped on one hilly island. Ram rivals off the cliffs and into the wreck, grab pickups, and stay ahead of the closing storm. Last truck running wins the island.

  • Drive: auto-accelerate; steer ←/→ or A/D; brake ↓/S. Jump (Shift) off ramps and ledges.
  • Boost (Space): refills over time + on golden pads/orbs. Boosted rams hit hard.
  • Storm closes in — anything caught outside it (or in the sea) takes damage. Wreck rivals or outlast them.
