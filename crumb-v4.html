Fortnite × Rocket League · 100-car battle royale

CRUMB ISLAND

100 cat-trucks rocket-drop onto a huge mountain island. Steer your fall, land, and loot — gear comes in rarities (common → rare → epic → legendary), and better loot bolts bigger upgrades onto your truck. Ram, flip, and outlast the storm.

  • Drive: auto-accel, steer ←/→, brake ↓/S, boost Space.
  • Rocket League moves: jump Shift; in the air, tap jump again to dodge-flip (a speed burst), or again for a double-jump. Strong air control on ramps + jump pads.
  • Loot rarity: common→rare→epic→legendary→MYTHIC. Armor/spikes/turbo/grip stack & show on the truck.
  • Gadgets (rocket pickups): fire with F. Oil slicks, machine guns, mines, smoke, homing missiles, EMP — rarer pickup = cooler weapon. MYTHIC = a 6-shot missile barrage.
